Healthly food - What is this?

Healthly food is a culinary project aimed at establishing healthy eating habits that will become an integral part of your life!

What do you get on the course?

Changing eating habits

We will find the causes of your breakdowns and malnutrition and get rid of them. We will advise you how to adjust your diet depending on your goals. We will help you get rid of cravings for sweets and build a proper diet.

80+ ready-made recipes for every day

You will learn how to prepare balanced meals quickly and deliciously. All cooking processes are described in detail, ingredients are selected and all the subtleties of cooking in the home kitchen are taken into account

Improving the quality of nutrition

Get support and guidance from expert nutritionists. The course is taught by two instructors - a chef with more than 10 years of experience and a professional nutritionist. You'll also have the support of like-minded people on your journey to healthy eating.
